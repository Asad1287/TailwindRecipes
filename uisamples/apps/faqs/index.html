SmartGarden FAQ

SmartGarden uses a combination of soil moisture sensors and weather predictions to determine the optimal watering schedule for your plants. The system measures the soil's moisture level in real-time and cross-references this data with upcoming weather forecasts to ensure your plants receive the right amount of water at the right time.

Yes, SmartGarden can adjust the nutrients in your soil. It uses a set of sensors to analyze the soil's nutrient composition and automatically dispenses the necessary fertilizers to maintain optimal growth conditions. You can customize the nutrient settings based on the specific needs of your plants through the SmartGarden app.

SmartGarden is designed to accommodate a wide variety of plant species. You can input the specific type of plant you are growing into the system, and it will adjust its watering, nutrient, and light settings accordingly. The SmartGarden app provides a database of plant species with pre-configured settings, making it easy to find the right parameters for your plants.
